Welcome to Ghazi's Deli

At Ghazi’s Deli, catering is an extension of how we serve our community every day — with fresh ingredients, homemade recipes, and care in every detail. Whether you’re planning a family gathering, office meeting, school event, or celebration, our zabiha halal catering options are prepared fresh and designed to please a variety of tastes. From crowd-favorite sandwiches to hearty platters and daily specials, we make it easy to feed your guests well.

As a family-run deli, we take pride in delivering food that’s not only delicious, but dependable and thoughtfully prepared. Everything is cooked right here using quality ingredients, generous portions, and honest pricing. When you choose Ghazi’s for catering, you’re choosing fresh food, friendly service, and a team that genuinely cares about making your event a success.

We proudly cater for a wide range of events, both large and small. Whether you’re hosting a professional gathering, celebrating a milestone, or planning an intimate family occasion, our catering menu is flexible and designed to fit your needs. With fresh, zabiha halal food and dependable service, Ghazi’s makes it easy to serve great meals no matter the setting or size of your event.
