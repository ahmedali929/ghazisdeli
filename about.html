About Us

A family-run halal deli built on fresh ingredients, homemade recipes, and care in every bite.

Deli   Overview

Our deli has been a part of the community since the 1950s. For decades, it was proudly family-run by an Italian family whose food, hospitality, and dedication turned the deli into a neighborhood favorite. Their legacy was built on simple values — fresh ingredients, homemade recipes, and treating every customer like family — values that are still felt and remembered today.

In 2024, the deli began a new chapter when it was passed on to another family eager to carry that legacy forward. With deep respect for the deli’s history, the new owners have embraced the responsibility of preserving what made this place special, while thoughtfully updating it for modern tastes and standards. By making the menu fully zabiha halal, the deli now welcomes people of all backgrounds and faiths to enjoy the same great food and warm atmosphere that have defined it for generations.

Today, the deli is run by a family who grew up right here in Connecticut and is proud to cook everything fresh on-site using quality ingredients. Every sandwich, meal, and special is prepared with care, honesty, and attention to detail. As we continue to grow, our goal is to become a true staple of the Connecticut food scene — honoring the past, serving the present, and always innovating for the future.




Why Ghazi's?

Fresh, homemade, and proudly halal — that’s the Ghazi’s difference. We’re a family-run deli committed to quality ingredients, honest portions, and great flavor. From breakfast sandwiches to daily specials, everything is made fresh and priced fairly. Once you try us, you’ll taste the difference.
